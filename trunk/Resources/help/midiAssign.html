MIDI Assignment


Assigning a MIDI controller to a slider is done in 2 steps:


1) Connect a MIDI controller that can output standard MIDI messages.


2) Right+click on the label of the slider you want to attach a MIDI Controller to. The "Move a MIDI controller" indicates that Cecilia is listening for any incoming controller informations.



3) Move the MIDI controller you want to assign to the slider. You have 10 seconds to move this MIDI controller.


Once assigned, the selected MIDI controller number appears in the slider knob.



NOTE At any moment, MIDI assignment of a slider can be changed by going over steps 2 and 3 again.
